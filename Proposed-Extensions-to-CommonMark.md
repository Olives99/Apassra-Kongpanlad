------------------------------------------------------------------------

```html5

<footer>

</footer>
```

------------------------------------------------------------------------

```html5
<p>text<a href="#sn1" class="sidenote marker" id="sn1.1">1</a></p>

<aside>
<p id="sn1">note, <a href="#sn1.1">↪︎</a></p>
</aside>
```

------------------------------------------------------------------------

```html5

</aside>
```
